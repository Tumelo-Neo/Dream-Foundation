Our Youth Programs

Discover the transformative programs we offer to empower young people in our community

Counseling Services

Supporting mental health and personal development

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with licensed therapists to address personal challenges and mental health concerns.

  • Confidential sessions
  • Trauma-informed care
  • LGBTQ+ affirming

Group Therapy

Support groups where youth can connect with peers facing similar challenges in a safe environment.

  • Peer support networks
  • Themed discussion groups
  • Skill-building workshops

Family Counseling

Services to improve family communication and resolve conflicts in a constructive way.

  • Mediation sessions
  • Parenting workshops
  • Reunification support

Career Support

Preparing youth for professional success

Youth Career Pathway Program

Our comprehensive career development program helps young people explore career options, develop job skills, and connect with employment opportunities.

Career Exploration

Discover various career paths through assessments and industry tours

Resume Building

Learn to create professional resumes and cover letters

Interview Prep

Practice interviews with feedback from professionals

Job Placement

Connections with employer partners and internship opportunities

Upcoming Career Workshops

Skills Development

Building practical skills for life and work

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, credit management, and financial planning workshops

Nutrition & Cooking

Healthy eating habits and basic cooking skills for independent living

Communication Skills

Developing effective verbal and non-verbal communication techniques

Coding Basics

Introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for beginners

Digital Productivity

Mastering office software and online collaboration tools

Online Safety

Protecting personal information and recognizing online threats

Visual Arts

Drawing, painting, and digital art workshops

Music Production
