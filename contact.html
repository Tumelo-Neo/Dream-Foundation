Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs

You can enroll in our programs by contacting our office directly via phone or email, or by visiting us during our open hours. Some programs may require an initial consultation or assessment. Visit our Activities page to learn more about specific program requirements.

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Some programs and counseling sessions may be available outside these hours by appointment.

Yes, we have staff members fluent in Spanish and Mandarin, and we can arrange interpretation services for other languages as needed. Please let us know your language needs when you contact us.

Most of our core programs are offered free of charge to youth participants, thanks to generous donations and grants. Some specialized workshops or materials may have nominal fees, but we never turn anyone away due to inability to pay.

We welcome volunteers in various capacities! Please visit our office to fill out a volunteer application, or contact our Volunteer Coordinator at volunteers@dreamfoundation.org. All volunteers undergo background checks and orientation training.
